Plasma Donation

Throughout your battle against COVID-19, your immune system developed antibodies against the virus. helped you to conquer the virus and now they are in your blood plasma.

If you fulfill all of the health criteria, then you can help those who are seriously ill to combat COVID-19 by making a donation. Or contribute to research efforts on antibodies.

Our ImmunHelden Map can help you find a suitable facility for plasma donations in your area.
